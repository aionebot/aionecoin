Aione Coin — Tokenomics & Distribution

Overview

Aione Coin (AIONE) is the native utility token of the Aione ecosystem — a unified infrastructure of AI services, privacy tools, digital assets, and Web3-enabled applications.
The token enables internal payments, subscriptions, intelligent services, user rewards, and interactions across the Aione product suite.

Token Information

Token Name: Aione Coin

Symbol: AIONE

Blockchain: Solana (Token-2022 standard)

Mint Address: AGd2dY72SqMtgsq7WBv6LLCoerER8VVfvGHsTn7wJGK9

Decimals: 6

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 AIONE

Mint Authority: Multisig (transferred)

Freeze Authority: Disabled

Metadata: On-chain Metadata Pointer (Token-2022)

Total Supply

The maximum supply of 1,000,000,000 AIONE is fully minted and permanently capped.
Additional minting is technically impossible due to the transfer of mint authority to a multisig wallet.
