## 1. Overview

**AIONE** is the native utility token of the Aione ecosystem — a multi-product platform focused on AI services, privacy tools, and fast Web3 payments.  
The token is implemented on the Solana blockchain using the Token-2022 standard.

AIONE powers:
- subscription payments,
- internal transactions,
- rewards and incentives,
- AI service credits,
- VPN and privacy services,
- future staking and governance modules.

The token is used exclusively for access to services and internal operations.
